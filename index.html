About Me

Hello, I'm Dinesh Panigrahi, an actor passionate about storytelling, performance and cinema. I hold a Master of Arts in Filmmaking from University of East London. My understanding of filmmaking, storytelling and performance shapes emotionally authentic and grounded work.


I have completed my workshop with The Actor's Truth and trained under veteran actor Saurabh Sachdeva. My training has strengthened my understanding of emotional truth, character development, screen presence and authentic performance.
